# AlMinhej Manifesto

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Draft

---

# One Sentence

> AlMinhej is a Knowledge Operating System that helps people discover, understand, connect, and live authentic Islamic knowledge while preserving the integrity of its original sources.

---

# Why We Exist

For over fourteen centuries, Islamic knowledge has been preserved through extraordinary scholarship.

The challenge today is no longer preserving knowledge.

The challenge is making authentic knowledge understandable and accessible without sacrificing its depth or integrity.

Modern learners often abandon classical texts not because they lack interest, but because they lack guidance.

AlMinhej exists to become that guide.

---

# What We Believe

Knowledge should inspire curiosity.

Learning should be calm.

Technology should remove friction.

Authenticity should never be compromised.

AI should assist, never replace scholarship.

Every learner deserves an entry point.

---

# What We Are NOT

AlMinhej is NOT:

- A Learning Management System
- A Course Platform
- A PDF Reader
- A Digital Library
- A Search Engine
- A Chatbot

---

# What We ARE

AlMinhej is a Knowledge Operating System.

Knowledge is the product.

Every feature exists to help users explore, understand and connect knowledge.

---

# Original Sources

Original Arabic texts always remain visible.

Translations are complementary.

Commentaries are distinguishable.

AI explanations are clearly separated.

Nothing replaces the source.

---

# Long-Term Vision

A learner begins with one hadith.

Without feeling overwhelmed they naturally discover:

- Quran
- Tafsir
- Arabic vocabulary
- Biography
- Historical events
- Jurisprudence
- Character
- Ethics
- Spirituality

Knowledge becomes a connected journey rather than isolated books.

---

# Guiding Question

Before implementing any feature ask:

> Does this help someone understand authentic knowledge better?

If the answer is "No", the feature does not belong in AlMinhej.
