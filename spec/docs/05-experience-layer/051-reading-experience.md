# ES-051 Reading Experience

Purpose

Create the calmest possible environment for understanding authentic knowledge.

---

Primary Goal

Understanding

Never speed.

Never information density.

---

Hierarchy

1 Original Arabic

2 Translation

3 Context

4 Commentary

5 Vocabulary

6 Reflection

7 Discovery

---

Rules

Arabic always remains visible.

Translations never replace Arabic.

Scholar commentary is clearly distinguished.

AI explanations are visually separated.

Reading progress is non-intrusive.

Animations never interrupt reading.

---

Reading Modes

Focused Reading

Guided Reading

Study Reading

Teacher Mode

Children Mode
