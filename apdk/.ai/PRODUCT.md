# PRODUCT

## Product Name

**AlMinhej**

---

# Product Definition

AlMinhej is an AI-native platform for preserving, engineering and exploring authentic Islamic knowledge.

The platform transforms authenticated Islamic books into an interconnected knowledge network while preserving the integrity and provenance of every original source.

It enables editors to build structured knowledge and enables readers to understand that knowledge through modern interactive experiences.

---

# Product Statement

Instead of creating another digital library...

AlMinhej creates the knowledge layer above authentic Islamic libraries.

Instead of replacing books...

AlMinhej reveals the relationships that already exist between them.

---

# Vision Statement

Every authenticated Islamic book should become a living digital knowledge network that is understandable, searchable, traceable and explorable while remaining faithful to its original source.

---

# Value Proposition

For readers:

> Understand authentic Islamic knowledge without losing connection to the original source.

For researchers:

> Navigate relationships that traditionally require consulting dozens of books.

For editors:

> Build reusable structured knowledge rather than isolated documents.

For institutions:

> Preserve scholarly work in a sustainable digital knowledge infrastructure.

# Product Principles

## Authenticity First

Every experience begins with authenticated sources.

---

## Knowledge Before Presentation

Knowledge is permanent.

Experiences are generated.

---

## Human Validation

AI assists.

Humans approve.

---

## Relationships Are Knowledge

Connections are first-class domain objects.

---

## Reuse Before Duplication

Knowledge should be referenced whenever possible.

The same biography, concept or historical event should not be recreated multiple times.

---

## Progressive Complexity

Beginners should see simple interfaces.

Experts should have access to complete detail.

The complexity belongs in the platform, not in the user interface.

# Out of Scope

The MVP does not include

- public community editing
- social networking
- discussion forums
- AI-generated religious rulings
- automated publishing
- mobile applications
- offline synchronization

These may be considered after the core knowledge platform has been validated.

---

# Product Summary

AlMinhej is not a content management system.

It is not a digital library.

It is not an educational course platform.

It is a knowledge engineering platform built upon authenticated Islamic sources.

Its purpose is to preserve, structure, connect and project knowledge while keeping the original source at the center of every experience.
